Server Rules & Guidelines

1. General Conduct

  • Always maintain professionalism when interacting with members
  • Be respectful and patient with all community members
  • Lead by example and follow all server rules

2. Ticket Management

  • Respond to tickets within 30 minutes when available
  • Always claim a ticket before responding
  • Use the AI assistant to help with common questions
  • Type "STOP" to pause AI and "START" to resume if needed
  • Escalate complex issues to senior staff

3. Moderation Guidelines

  • Issue warnings before taking harsher actions
  • Document all moderation actions in the appropriate channel
  • Consult with other staff for serious violations

4. AI System Usage

  • The AI learns from all server conversations
  • Staff can teach the AI specific responses using the /teach command
  • AI operations can be controlled via the dashboard
  • Review AI responses periodically for accuracy
